About Me

Thank you for taking a look. This static website project is meant to showcase some general information about myself and some of the projects and activities that I am involved in. Hopefully there is something here that intrigues you or makes you smile.

I am a student pursing a Computer Engineering degree at the University of Waterloo. Originally I pursued engineering because I was good at the math and the sciences but eventually became fascinated by the technology field and its immense ability to change the world we live in. I am trying hard to become a part of this change and I hope to showcase some of my endeavours below.

My curiosity and interests brought me to the field of programming but also took me beyond the computer lab to many of my hobbies and pastimes. I love challenging myself and relaxing at the gym - I think it's a great way of balancing out the amount of time I spend in front of my computer screen. In addition, I am a massive fan of basketball and I greatly enjoy spending time with friends on the court and following NBA basketball. I also enjoy listening to music from various genres ranging from kpop to rap and hip hop as well as a fair share of books, movies and dramas. If I have the time, I am an avid adventurer who loves to visit the outdoors and travel the world with my family and friends.

When I'm not working on a project or reading tech tutorials, I love thinking about and playing around with any small thoughts or ideas that I come up with. I am incredibly passionate about starting challenging projects and tackling difficult problems- I hope that my passion for solving problems and learning will continue to be a central focus for my career in the future.
