About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I'm all about working to improve myself and I have a particular interest in the technology industry and its immense ability to change the world we live in. I aspire to be a part of this change and I am learning more about this field every day. I am passionately curious and a strong and motivated problem solver.

My curiosity and interests brought me to many of my recent programming projects but also took me beyond the lab to explore many other topics. I've taken an interest in the human body and learning how to stay healthy and I love spending time at the gym and the basketball court working on my jumpshot. Occasionally I would also pick up my guitar and alto saxophone but my skills are currently still works in progress. I enjoy music from various genres ranging from kpop to rap and hip hop as well as a fair share of books, movies and dramas. If I have the time, I am an avid adventurer who loves to visit the outdoors and travel the world with my family and friends.

When I'm not working on a project or reading tech tutorials, I love thinking about and playing around with small ideas that I have. I am incredibly passionate about starting challenging projects and tackling difficult problems- I hope that my passion for solving problems and learning will continue to be a central focus for my career in the future
