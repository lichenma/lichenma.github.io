About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I'm all about working to improve myself and I have a particular interest in the technology industry and its immense ability to change the world we live in. I aspire to be a part of this change and I am eagerly learning more about this field every day. I am passionately curious and a strong problem solver.

I am an eager learner and I strive to learn new things in all facets of life. I am a fitness enthusiast with a particular love for basketball (Go Spurs!). Occasionally I would pick up my guitar and alto saxophone and fly through books but I haven't been able to keep up as much as I want to. I enjoy music from various genres ranging from kpop to rap and hip hop as well as a fair share of movies and dramas. If I have the time, I love to visit the outdoors and travel the world with my family and friends- I have been getting a lot more into hiking and boating in the beautiful parks around Canada.

When I'm not working on a project or reading tech tutorials, I love thinking about and playing around with small ideas that I have. I am incredibly passionate about starting challenging projects and tackling difficult problems- I hope that my passion for solving problems and learning will continue to be a central focus for my career in the future
